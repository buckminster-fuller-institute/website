From the Idea Index



The Perseus. A floating, inhabited sea station self-sufficient in food, water and energy.
2010

To dissipate fears of increasing food and water limitations, while promoting momentum for worldwide responsible sea stewardship, we will establish, inhabit, and widely publicize a floating eco-friendly marine station, self-sufficient in food, water and energy, thereby showing the wealth of opportunities waiting to be intelligently developed at sea.
