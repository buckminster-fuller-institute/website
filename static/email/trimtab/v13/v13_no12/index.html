Shore Soup Provides On-going Sandy Relief with Hot Meals

Shore Soup is a comprehensive support system for those still lacking power and heat after Hurricane Sandy. The team behind Shore Fruit switched gears to deliver greatly-needed hot meals around the hurricane affected area. Those in the New York area can help out by volunteering or donating food, and those further afield can provide financial support on the project's website.

Living Future Institute's Call for Entries

The Living Future Institute has released their call for proposals to lead an education session at their conference in May 2013. Applicants are asked to respond to this year's theme of Resilience & Regeneration. Read more about how to answer the call here!



Trillions: Thriving in the Information Ecology Released

Peter Lucas, Joe Bailay, and Mickey McManus have released a "field guide to the future", a book discussing the complex, intangible impact that networked computers and microprocessors will have in the years to come. The book asks "whether that complexity is harnessed to build a world that is safe, pleasant, humane, and profitable, or whether it causes us to careen off a cliff into an abyss of mind-numbing junk..." See more about the book here.



Think Beyond Plastic Competition accepting proposals

Think Beyond Plastic is soliciting proposals to move beyond our reliance on plastic, with specific attention to applications addressing supply chain and infrastructure, source materials and packaging, products, services or business model innovations. There's a lot of potential for innovative projects, read more here!
