Great Summer Read!
A Fuller Explanation: The Synergetic Geometry of R. Buckminster Fuller by Amy C. Edmondson

This book is excellent summer reading material for any math student with a curiosity about Fuller's 'Geometry of Thinking.' This new edition revives an indispensable classic, originally described as "the long-awaited and outstanding new book that explains Buckminster Fuller's Synergetics. The author, who worked closely with Fuller the last three years of his life, realized that the 1400 pages of dense prose in Fuller's Synergetics can be explained in clear terms."
