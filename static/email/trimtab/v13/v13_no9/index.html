Boing Boing partner, David Pescovitz, writes a heartfelt tribute to Bucky and "the importance of unintended consequences", accompanied by some beautiful and rare images. Read it here!

John Cage Turns 100

John Cage would have turned 100 this year on September 5th. He and Bucky share a creative lineage through the Black Mountain College. At the BFI, we enjoyed taking the occasion to look back at his work. Check out a staff favorite of Cage's 100th Birthday reporting, from NPR, here.

Ornette: Made In America rereleased

For another look at Bucky's influence on music, check out this free-form documentary on jazz pioneer, Ornette Coleman, who lists Bucky as his chief artistic inspiration. Click here for a New Yorker review of the film.

Tesla Fans Leap Into Action!

As we here know, great geeks have great supporters: When cartoonist Matthew Inman started an online funding campaign to turn Tesla's lab, Wardenclyffe into a museum, fans rushed to action, funding the project in 6 days. Help them out here!
