From the Idea Index



Ecosistema Urbano
2011

Ecosistema Urbano have designed and applied a water purification system that works by natural means (macrophites). The system is able to transform the waste waters of buildings into purified water that can be reused for different purposes. We use the residual or spare city spaces ( roundabouts) to place the water treatment system.
